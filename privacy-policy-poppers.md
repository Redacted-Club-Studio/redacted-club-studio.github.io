# Privacy Policy — Popper

**Last updated: March 2026**

Redacted Club Studio ("we", "us") operates the mobile game **Popper** (the "App").

---

## Information We Collect

We do not directly collect any personal information.

The App uses **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data including:

- Advertising ID (a resettable device identifier)
- IP address
- General location (country/region)
- Device information (model, OS version)
- Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with their policies.

---

## How It Is Used

Data collected via AdMob is used solely to serve relevant advertisements. We do not sell or share any user data.

## Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
